About

Hip-Hop Meets Its Origin Story

The Rich Flip is a live producer battle honoring Little Richard’s musical legacy through modern beat culture. Producers flip his catalog in real time while artists, DJs, and visual creators build a shared cultural experience in Macon, Georgia.
